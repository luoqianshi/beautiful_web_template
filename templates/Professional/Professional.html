The Craft

Built for those who treat writing as architecture

Every feature is considered with the same care a typographer gives to kerning. Nothing is added without intention.

Editorial Typography Engine

A complete system of high-contrast serifs, tracked small caps, and refined rule lines. Set the tone of a printed magazine, online.

Considered Versioning

Track every revision with marginal annotations. Restore any moment with the precision of a master editor's red pen.

Global by Design

Publish in 189 countries with native typographic conventions. Roman, Cyrillic, and CJK sets, all kerned by hand.

Grid & Rule System

Compose layouts on classical modular grids with thin rule lines that structure without weighing the page down.

Marginal Annotations

Leave notes in the margins exactly as editors have for centuries. Threads stay tethered to the line they discuss.

Quiet Automation

House styles applied invisibly in the background. The machinery disappears so the writing can breathe.
