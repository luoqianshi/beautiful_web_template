FAQ

Questions, carved in

Answers feel pressed into the surface of each card.

What makes Neumorphism accessible?
Our foreground color #3D4852 on the #E0E5EC background achieves a 7.5:1 contrast ratio (WCAG AAA). Muted text at #6B7280 still meets 4.6:1 (WCAG AA). All interactive elements have visible 2px accent focus rings.
Why RGBA shadows instead of hex?
RGBA transparency allows the light and dark shadows to blend smoothly with the background, creating realistic, soft lighting. Solid hex shadows produce harsh, unnatural edges that break the molded-surface illusion.
Can I customize the accent color?
Yes. The default soft violet (#6C63FF) and teal (#38B2AC) are CSS custom properties. Override them globally or per-component. Keep the monochromatic background discipline—shadows do the visual heavy lifting.
Is it responsive on mobile?
Fully mobile-first. Touch targets meet 44px minimums, grids collapse gracefully, and the neumorphic aesthetic is maintained across all breakpoints with a hamburger menu below the md breakpoint.
