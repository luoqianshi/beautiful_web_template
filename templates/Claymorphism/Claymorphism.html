Features

Every element bulges with life.

Premium digital clay with complex, multi-layered lighting simulation. Surfaces absorb light rather than reflecting it sharply, creating a warm, inviting aesthetic.

High-Fidelity Shadow Stacks

Every element uses carefully crafted 4-layer shadow stacks: outer drop shadows, top-left highlights, inner reflections, and active-state insets. This renders objects that feel dense, substantial, and interactive — not hollow decorations.

Candy-Store Palette

Vivid violets, hot pinks, sky blues, and emerald greens radiate joy and optimism through saturated, harmonious color relationships.

Zero Sharp Corners

Every edge is aggressively rounded — 20px minimum, up to 60px for large containers — signaling safety and approachability without words.

Tactile Micro-Physics

Buttons actively squish (scale 0.92 + pressed shadow) and cards lift towards you. Every interaction provides satisfying visual feedback.

Buoyant Animation

The interface exists in zero gravity with high air resistance. Background blobs drift slowly, cards hover effortlessly — everything breathes and moves subtly.

Process

Three steps to tangible interfaces.

Convexity, concavity, and buoyancy — the clay physics engine in three movements.

1

The Bulge

Primary elements bulge out with convex shadows, capturing light on top-left edges and casting soft colored shadows below.

2

The Press

Secondary surfaces press into the clay with inset shadows, casting internal shadows on top edges and catching light on bottom lips.

3

The Float

Everything exists in zero gravity. Cards hover with amplified float effects on hover — nothing feels statically stuck to the grid.
