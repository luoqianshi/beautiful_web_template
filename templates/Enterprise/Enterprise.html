FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is there a free plan?
Yes. The Starter plan is free forever and includes up to 5 team members, 3 active workflows, and 1GB of storage. No credit card required.
Can I change plans later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your billing settings. Changes take effect immediately and we prorate the difference.
Which security certifications do you have?
We are SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, and HIPAA compliant. SSO/SAML, audit logs, and granular role-based access control are available on the Enterprise plan.
Do you offer support?
Community support is available on all plans. Growth plans include priority email support, and Enterprise customers get a dedicated Customer Success Manager and 24/7 response SLAs.
