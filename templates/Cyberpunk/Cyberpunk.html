FEATURE_MATRIX

High-tech. Low-life.

The aesthetic is a digital dystopia colliding with a high-tech noir reality. Every pixel feels like it's being rendered on a malfunctioning CRT monitor in a rain-soaked alley.

Chromatic Aberration

RGB color splitting on text and elements simulates lens distortion and signal interference. The UI feels like it's struggling to contain the data it displays.

Scanline Overlay

Subtle horizontal line overlays mimic the refresh rate of old CRT monitors, adding texture and unifying the composition across the entire page.

Terminal Aesthetic

Monospace fonts, > prefixes, and blinking cursor animations. Sections feel like a rogue terminal in a subterranean bunker.

Neon Glow

Text and borders literally glow with multi-layered box-shadow stacking, creating a light-saber effect against the dark void background.

Glitch Effects

Intentional corruption via clip-path animations, skewed transforms, and flickering text suggests an unstable connection or a hacked system.

Corner Cuts

Chamfered corners via clip-path replace friendly rounded rectangles. Borders are technical, precise — resembling blueprints or HUD schematics.
