DOCUMENTATION

Frequently asked

Is Satoshi non-custodial?▾
Yes. Your private keys never leave your wallet. We use smart contracts that you interact with directly. We cannot move your funds, freeze your account, or access your collateral — even if legally compelled. Your keys, your coins.
How are yields generated?▾
Yield comes from on-chain strategies: lending to blue-chip borrowers, providing concentrated liquidity to audited pools, and staking to validated validators. Every strategy is documented, audited, and visible on-chain. No opaque off-chain activity.
What happens during a market crash?▾
Positions are over-collateralized by default. If your collateral ratio drops, you receive notifications at 80%, 75%, and 70%. Liquidation only triggers at 65%, and the $480M insurance fund covers any shortfall. No user has lost principal in 5 years.
Can I verify the reserves myself?▾
Yes. Proof-of-reserves is published on-chain every block. We use Merkle tree attestations — you can cryptographically verify your specific balance is included in the total, without revealing your identity. Tools and guides are in our docs.
What chains are supported?▾
14 chains including Ethereum, Bitcoin (via wrapping), Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, Polygon, Avalanche, Solana, and BNB Chain. Cross-chain deposits settle natively — no synthetic tokens, no wrapped intermediaries held by us.
