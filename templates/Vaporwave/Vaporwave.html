Frequently Queried

root@neondrive:~/help$
Is this just a CSS theme?▼
No. It is a complete design system with grid engines, gradient libraries, skewed kinetics, CRT overlays, and glass morphism utilities. A philosophy, not a stylesheet.
Will the neon glows hurt performance?▼
All effects use GPU-accelerated CSS (box-shadow, filter, backdrop-filter). We benchmark at 60fps on mid-range hardware. Scanlines and grids are static overlays with zero runtime cost.
Does it work on mobile?▼
Yes. Neon glows, borders, and grid backgrounds are non-negotiable on mobile. Glows are slightly reduced to prevent overwhelming small screens, but the aesthetic survives intact.
Can I disable the CRT scanlines?▼
You can, but you would be missing the point. Scanlines are the soul of the aesthetic. If readability is critical, reduce opacity to 0.15 — they remain visible without compromising text.
Why so much magenta?▼
Hot magenta (#FF00FF) is THE hero color. It is a neon tube glowing in a dark void. Paired with electric cyan, it creates the maximum-saturation contrast that defines vaporwave.
