Features that feel handcrafted

Every tool built to keep your brain in that messy, generative flow state.

Wobbly Canvas

An infinite canvas with organic, hand-drawn grid lines. Draw, erase, smudge — everything wobbles just a little.

Marker Tools

Eight marker styles that feel like the real thing — felt-tip, pencil, highlighter, ballpoint. Each with authentic texture.

Sticky Notes

Drag, drop, stack, and crumple digital Post-its. Color-code your chaos. Tape them anywhere on the board.

Co-Doodling

Invite the whole team. Everyone's cursor leaves a little colored trail. It's chaotic in the best way.

Export Anywhere

Turn your scribbles into PNGs, PDFs, or shareable links. Keep the wobble or clean it up — your call.

Autosave Magic

Your doodles save themselves every few seconds. Close the laptop, open it tomorrow, chaos preserved.

★ why scribble?

Imperfection is the point

★ the manifesto ★
Wobbly borders reject the cold perfection of modern UI — your brain relaxes.
Hand-drawn doodles lower the barrier to putting ideas down fast.
Paper textures and tape strips make the digital feel physical.
Playful rotation breaks rigid grids and invites creative chaos.
Hard offset shadows give every element a cut-paper, collage feel.
Limited palette — pencil, paper, red marker, post-it — keeps focus on ideas.
