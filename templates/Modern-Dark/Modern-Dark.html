FAQ

Frequently asked

How fast is Linearis, really?
Every interaction targets sub-50ms with optimistic UI updates. We benchmark continuously against a baseline of native desktop apps, so the web interface feels instant — no spinners, no waiting, just response.
Can I migrate from Jira or Linear?
Yes. Our one-click importer pulls in projects, issues, labels, and history from Jira, Linear, Asana, and GitHub Projects. Most teams complete migration in under an hour with zero data loss.
Is my data secure?
We're SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and encrypt all data at rest and in transit. Enterprise plans add SSO, SCIM, and granular audit logs for full organizational control.
Do you offer a discount for startups?
Absolutely. Seed and Series A companies get 50% off the Business plan for the first year. Reach out to our team with your funding details and we'll get you set up.
