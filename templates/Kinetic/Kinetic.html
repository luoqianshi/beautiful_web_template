— Features

What
Moves
You

01

Infinite Marquees

GPU-accelerated scrolling that never stops. No gradients — raw, continuous motion with hairline dividers. Fast for stats, slower for reading.

02

Viewport Typography

Fluid scaling using clamp() from 3rem to 14rem. Headlines fill the screen. Viewport-width units create immediate scale and drama.

03

Hard Inversions

Cards completely flip color scheme on hover — black to yellow, white to black. Clean transitions, not gradual. The brutalist way.

04

Scroll Triggered

Scale and opacity transforms fire as you scroll. Hero zooms out, cards stack and overlap. Every element feels alive through reactive motion.
