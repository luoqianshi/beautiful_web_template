FAQ

Questions, answered calmly

Is Nocturne only for night owls?
Not at all. The ambient design adapts to your environment. Many teams use it during the day for its calm, focused aesthetic — the dark palette simply reduces eye strain at any hour.
Does the glass effect impact performance?
Backdrop blur is GPU-accelerated and highly optimized on modern devices. We carefully tune ambient orbs and blur radius to maintain a smooth 60fps even on mid-range hardware.
Can I customize the amber accent?
Pro and Enterprise plans unlock accent theming. You can choose from a curated palette of warm tones, all tuned to glow beautifully against the deep slate background.
What about data security?
We're SOC 2 Type II certified with end-to-end encryption. Your data sleeps as soundly as you do — ambient monitoring watches over it 24/7 without ever compromising privacy.
